EcoConnect (Social Service App)

EcoConnect is an innovative mobile application designed to promote environment sustainability living, facilitate various types of donations, support fundraisers, and connect volunteers through social media features. The app aims to create a community of environmentally conscious individuals and organizations working together to make a positive impact on society and the planet.

KinView (Parental App)

The Kinview app is designed to enhance parental engagement and ensure child safety by providing live CCTV monitoring, an instant SOS call feature for emergencies, detailed tracking of daily activities, and timely notifications about upcoming events, all aimed at fostering a stronger connection between parents and their children's educational experience.
